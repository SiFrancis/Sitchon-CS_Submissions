FOUNDATION FOR MEDIA ALTERNATIVES

One of the leading non-profits in the Philippines tackling online privacy, human rights, and freedom of expression.

    WHY SHOULD YOU JOIN THIS CHARITY?

  • The FMA works to promote various human rights issues such as gender rights, privacy, and freedom of expression in a digital era. They seek to democratize information and communication technology (ICT) such as the Internet for citizens and communities in need, and have been doing so since their formation in 1987.
  • Currently, they are focused on three main advocacies: Gender and ICT, privacy and data protection, and internet rights.
  • In 2018, the FMA ran a series of workshops for university students on Internet feminism and the Feminist Principles of the Internet, which touch on a broad range of issues related to feminism, sexual rights, and LGBTQIA+ empowerment.
  • The FMA also writes State of Privacy reports that detail privacy issues in the Philippines. They also publish various resources on these privacy issues such as briefing papers and infographics.
  • The FMA has partnered with international organizations such as Global Partners Digital and the Association for Progressive Communications on various initiatives, such as the reports and resources mentioned above. The FMA has also partnered with local Internet-focused organizations and charities to create the Philippine Declaration on Internet Rights and Principles.
