JOIN US

    There are many ways you could help us in a journey to a more connected world. Here are just a few:



MAKE A PETITION

    One of HyperLink's main intiatives include pushing laws with the Internet in mind; and we could really use your help. Here are several petition forms that you can send to your local officials to promote our cause.


USE OPEN SOURCE

    Open source software takes all the apps and services you love, and removes all the corporate bloat and spyware. Using open source software gives you a better, more private, and more secure digital experience.



VOLUNTEER

    If you are interested to join one of HyperLink's many projects and initiatives, feel free to fill up this volunteer form! If you have any concerns or queries, don't hesitate to reach out to us via email or social media. There are many ways that you can help as a volunteer!

DONATE

    Monetary donations to our cause are greatly appreciated. Any interested donors may fill up this form, which includes instructions for all accepted payment methods. HyperLink releases an annual Transparency Report to ensure that your donations are going to the right places!
