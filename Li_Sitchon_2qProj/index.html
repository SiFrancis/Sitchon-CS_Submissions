The digital world is rapidly evolving.
But is the real world keeping up?


    Everyone can agree that the Internet is ubiquitous nowadays. From our entertainment to work to shopping, the Internet influences our lives every day.

    However, it is not as ubiquitous as you may think. About 40% of the world still has no access to the Internet. Even in the places that do, poorer countries such as those in Africa and South Asia only have 30-40% of their population who actively use the Internet; contrast this with 92% of the population in North America being Internet users.



We are here to change all that.
